Discover Heart of Italy, Umbria

Umbria is a landlocked region in central Italy, bordered by Tuscany, Marche, and Lazio, and is often referred to as the "Green Heart of Italy" due to its lush landscapes of hills, forests, valleys, and lakes such as Lake Trasimeno and the Marmore Falls. It is the only Italian region without a coastline or a border with another country. It is the only Italian region without a coastline or a border with another country.

Recipe for Tiramisu

Tiramisu is an Italian dessert

Ingredients:

  • 300g Ladyfingers(Naples Biscuits)
  • 4 Fresh Eggs
  • 100-110g Caster Sugar
  • 300ml espresso Coffee
  • 500g Mascarpone
  • Few spoon of Cocoa powder

Instructions:

  1. Prepare strong coffee and let it cool.
  2. Whisk egg yolks with sugar, then gently add mascarpone.
  3. Whip egg whites with a pinch of salt and fold them into the cream.
  4. In a dish, layer coffee-dipped ladyfingers and mascarpone cream alternately.
  5. Finish with cream on top, dust with cocoa powder, and chill at least 5 hours.
